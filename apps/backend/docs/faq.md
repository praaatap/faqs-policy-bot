# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## 1. How do I request a leave?
You can request leave through the HR Portal. Log in, select "Leave Request", choose the dates, and submit. Your manager will receive an automated notification.

## 2. When is payday?
Payments are processed on the last business day of each month. Pay slips are available in the Finance portal.

## 3. Can I use my own laptop?
Yes, but it must be registered with the IT Security team. They will install the required security software and VPN access.

## 4. What is the referral bonus?
We offer a $2,000 bonus for every successful referral who completes 6 months of employment. The bonus is paid out in the next pay cycle after the 6-month milestone.

## 5. Who do I contact for IT support?
Email `support@company.com` or open a ticket on the IT Helpdesk. For urgent hardware issues, visit the IT desk on the 2nd floor.
