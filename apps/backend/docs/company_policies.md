# Company Policies

## 1. Work Hours
Our standard office hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. We offer flexible working hours as long as core hours (10:00 AM to 4:00 PM) are covered.

## 2. Remote Work
Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Home office setup stipend is provided once every two years.

## 3. Leave Policy
- **Annual Leave**: 25 business days per year.
- **Sick Leave**: 10 paid sick days. Medical certificate required for more than 3 consecutive days.
- **Parental Leave**: 16 weeks of fully paid leave for the primary caregiver.

## 4. Code of Conduct
We maintain a professional environment free from harassment. All employees must follow the ethical guidelines outlined in the Employee Handbook.

## 5. Expenses
Business-related expenses must be pre-approved. Claims should be submitted within 30 days of the expense.
